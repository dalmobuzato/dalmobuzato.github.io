This dataset of signs produced by Warao refugees in Brazil was created and grammatically annotated by Dalmo Buzato and Átila Vital, students, and researchers at the Federal University of Minas Gerais. All photographs presented were collected by the authors, or have authorization for use for academic and scientific purposes.
